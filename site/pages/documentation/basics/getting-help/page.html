There are several channels to reach the developers and the Flextype community to get direct assistance.

If you can't find the right answers to your questions, you can turn to one of our other sources to get help and discuss your issues, but also to receive news and update notifications.

Forum

A passionate Flextype user has started a forum for the community to discuss and share their knowledge.

Gitter

Gitter is a chat tool that is tightly coupled with GitHub. If you don't already have one, you will need to create a free GitHub account. Once done, you can access the chat at https://gitter.im/flextype/flextype or you can download one of the available Gitter apps.

Github Issues

If you want to report a bug or contribute your ideas, you can use the Flextype GitHub Issues tracker.

Twitter

Follow Flextype on Twitter to get real-time news regarding the development and all events we are attending.

Blog

Read major project news and articles for developers on the flextype Blog. Stay up to date with new releases and join the discussion in the comments section.
